We may collect personal details such as name, email address, and contact number when voluntarily submitted through sign-up forms, contact requests, or other interactions within our digital marketing training portal.

Information collected may be used for the following purposes:

  1. Deliver personalized digital marketing training content and resources;

By using our digital marketing training platform, you acknowledge and accept this Privacy Policy and its terms.

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice. Any revisions will be posted here and become effective immediately. Users are encouraged to review this policy periodically for the latest information.

For any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at skill_clicks@gmail.com.
