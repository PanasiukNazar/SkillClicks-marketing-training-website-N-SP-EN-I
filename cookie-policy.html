Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies on our platform to enhance your experience during your digital marketing training journey.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us remember your preferences, improve navigation, and analyze usage patterns to make your learning experience more effective.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Support login and user authentication for training platform access;
  • Remember your course preferences and settings;
  • Track learning progress and usage for platform improvement;
  • Provide relevant content based on your behavior;
  • Analyze site traffic and interaction through analytics tools.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Our platform uses the following categories of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for basic functions like login and course access.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our platform.
  • Functionality Cookies: Enable enhanced features such as saving course preferences.
  • Analytics Cookies: Allow us to gather insights for improving our training content.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services, such as analytics providers, to collect and analyze data. These services may place their own cookies subject to their privacy policies.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. However, disabling certain cookies may impact your ability to access some training features.

6. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our digital marketing training platform evolves. Updates will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at skill_clicks@gmail.com.

By continuing to use our platform, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy.
